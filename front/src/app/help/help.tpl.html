What is ShopNow?

ShopNow is a mobile app that helps you find great offers & products updates from brands you like. It allows you to send a request to the store, and receive a discount or offer personalised just for you.

Read more here...

How do I get discounts?

All the brands you love are able to deliver offers and discount, and you will be immediately notified when the offer or discount is ready to use.

When the offers are ready for you, we will send a push notification to your smartphone. You can always check all available offers in the “Offers” tab in your mobile app.

Who founded ShopNow?

ShopNow was co-founded by Chris Witko and Wojciech Krupa.

Currently, we are working on this functionality and we will launch it in the near future.

How long do I have to wait to receive my requested discount?

It depends, how active the brand is on ShopNow. We monitor every request and aim to deliver discounts quickly. Because we’re still growing, it might take a little while longer to get back to you with your discount.

Can I unsubscribe selected brands?

Yes. It’s easy. Just visit the brand you would like to unsubscribe and click “…” button. Select “Unsubscribe”.

When I unsubscribe from selected brand, do I automatically unlike this page on Facebook?

No. We do not touch your likes on Facebook. If you will unsubscribe some brands on ShopNow, they will stay as your liked brands on Facebook.

Where to find who’s of my friends is already using ShopNow?

Open the “Settings” tab, and select “Facebook Friends”

Why I can’t register using my email address?

Our app is using your Facebook profile to learn what stores or brands you are interested in, and deliver the best results. Using only email address we are unable to deliver a fantastic service.

Why do you need all my data from Facebook?

Currently, we only support users who wants to sign in using their Facebook account. We only request your data like: Likes, Birthdate, Friends. ShopNow uses this to deliver the best service for you.

Help for Business

How can ShopNow help my business?

Our mission is to give users quick and easy access to follow all their favourite brands all in one app. Using our app users are able to communicate with your brand by requesting discounts, following recent posts and receiving personalised offers. For you it’s a cheaper way than mailing to communicate with your customers.

How much does it cost?

You can use ShopNow for Free. With our Free package you can send one offer to subscribers. If you want to have access to statistics, and the ability to send more, you’ll have to upgrade to our Basic package. The Basic package costs £19 per month.

How do I become a merchant partner?

Visit our page for business, and use your Facebook profile to register for ShopNow. After successful registration you will see all your Facebook pages you administer on Facebook.

If you have questions, please contact info@shopnowapp.com.

What are Moments?

Our Moments feature allows you to create highly personal offers that are delivered to your users at special times. For instance, you can surprise them with a celebratory offer on their birthday, or welcome them the first time they sign up.

Why the maximum offer time is 30 days?

Currently we allow to post offer valid maximum for 30 day.

Can I schedule custom time for offers?

We are working to make this option flexible in the future.

What do I need to start using ShopNow?

Only Facebook account and pages you administer.

How Does The Free Trial Work?

It’s completely free, no credit card required. Just register with your Facebook profile and you can use Free version of ShopNow.

Why We Can’t Stop or Edit Sent Offers

Once an offer has been sent, it cannot be stopped or edited since the content has already been sent to the servers.

How Can I Pay For ShopNow?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Diners, Discover, JCB. We use Stripe to support payments.

Do I need to pay VAT?

Price is VAT exclusive. VAT will be added for EU customers who have not provided a valid VAT EU number and for all customers registered in the United Kingdom.

What Happens When I Cancel A Paid Account?

You can always use Free package only if you need more time to test our app. If you are on the Basic package, you can always stop subscription in your account settings.

Need More Help Before Signing Up?

If you have questions about ShopNow, our pricing, features or the sign-up process, please feel free to contact us at info@shopnowapp.com — we’re always here to help you!
